The Lion King Arts Contest 2024

Welcome to the 2024 Lion King Arts Contest Portal

Description of the contest:

Contestants will get to create an alternate ending for The Lion King (1994) film. Whether using arts (like a comic book strip), writing, or both, contestants will be tasked to complete this project. To win, creativity is key. The board will choose the winner based on the most compelling design and captivating storyline, which will be featured on this site as the winner!

Please submit your creation by Friday, September 6th, by 11:59 PM!
